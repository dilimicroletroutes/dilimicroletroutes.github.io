What are Microlets?

Microlets are mini buses that make up the public transport of Dili, the capital of Timor-Leste. These microlets are colour-coded and numbered. Each ride costs 25 centavos (local coins equivalent to USD$0.25). There are no bus stops in Dili. To stop a microlet, flag it along its route and hop on when it stops! When you are about to arrive at your destination, tap your 25c on any metal railings around you.
