About Us

Claudia Gee, Geographer

I was in Timor-Leste for two months (May to July) in 2018 on an internship during my semester holidays. My purpose of being in Timor was to help to map and zone Nino Konis Santana National Park. I finished my assignment early and during my last 3 weeks in Timor, I was thinking of ways to give back to the community for being so welcoming during my stay here.

This website was created with tourists or anyone new to Dili in mind. When I arrived in Dili late May 2018, I wanted to learn to use the public transport. I came across www.dilimicrolets.com and I was thrilled. Although extremely informative, I came across some difficulties when I tried to use it on-the-go. The need to toggle between Google Maps (to search where my destination is located and also for location services to find where I was) and www.dilimicrolets.com under the sweltering heat or in the moving microlet was challenging. That was how Dili Microlet Routes begun!

I hope my project has helped you one way or another in exploring Dili!
